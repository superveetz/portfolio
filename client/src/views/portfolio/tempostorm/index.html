Tempostorm

A large-scale AngularJS application served with NodeJS. We used LoopBack.io as our back-end framework in conjunction with MongoDB to manage large quantities of data efficiently.

Tempostorm is one of the most well-known organizations in competitive online video gaming scene. They sponsor dozens of players across the world to compete internationally in some of the most popular video games. Their website allows content-providers to write new articles and guides daily to maintain fresh content for their fans and I helped build the tools to allow them to do this.

My main responsibilities included writing the front-end AngularJS code. I assisted in overhauling the site to use a new back-end framework by reprogramming all of the database queries to our new API as well as improved the UX where possible. Furthermore, I was responsible for modeling data and implementing new features while also extending the functionality of existing features.
